Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Protect your confidential information when discussing your startup with potential investors, partners, or employees.

Terms of Service

Establish the rules and guidelines for users of your website or application, covering topics like acceptable use, liability, and dispute resolution.

Privacy Policy

Inform users about how you collect, use, and protect their personal data, ensuring compliance with privacy regulations.

Independent Contractor Agreement

Clearly define the terms of your working relationship with independent contractors, including scope of work, payment terms, and ownership of deliverables.

Employment Agreement

Formalize the terms of employment with your employees, covering aspects like job responsibilities, compensation, benefits, and termination conditions.
